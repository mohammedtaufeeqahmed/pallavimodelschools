About

Pallavi Educational society

“Knowledge is Liberation”. With a motto to engender exceptional students who in turn make an astonishing society to live in, the Pallavi educational society has initiated Pallavi Model School in June 1994. With high standards of academics clubbed with advanced curriculum with various facilities Sports, Libraries, Labs, Arts and more, Pallavi Keesara, the Best Schools in Hyderabad, has evolved into a prosperous Educational Institute in the past 30 years.

With a legacy of 30 Years, we stand tall among the best schools in Hyderabad with our wings spread across 10 schools in twin cities. Apart from academic excellence, we additionally accentuate on multi-faceted development of the child by examining and understanding the children fascinates and inspiriting them to reach the zenith of prosperity. We believe that if parents and teachers cumulate, we can harness the ebullience of our children and guide them to be self-ample and useful members of society.

Mission

To impart holistic learning in a safe, secure and joyful learning environment. Where respect, tolerance, honesty and individual differences are appreciated. To develop leaders who shape the world through independent, creative and critical thinking.


Vision

Pallavi Group of Schools envisages creating socially responsible ethical, global leaders with academic excellence and quest for perpetual learning.


Core Values

To be a dynamic institution in equilibrium, we nurture the following values:

  • 1 Righteousness
  • 2 Collaboration
  • 3 Innovation
  • 4 Eco-friendly
  • 5 Respect for Nation and individuals

Pallavi Group of Schools are one of the best schools in Hyderabad was established in the year 1994, with a motto to create exceptional students who excel in their career and every aspect of their life.
