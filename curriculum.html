Curriculum

Pallavi Model school - one of the prestigious CBSE Schools in Hyderabad, believe that education during the formative years should primarily focus on discipline, right conduct, truthfulness, devotion, and duty mindedness. All these principles are blended into the pedagogical practices followed by the teachers along with a strong academic base.


The various co-curricular activities and strong teaching faculty are instrumental in helping us grow leaps and bounds in the last 29+ years, which is evident through the various branches we currently have.
