Why Us

Why Pallavi Group

Pallavi Group of Schools began its glorious journey with its first branch – Pallavi Model School, Bowenpally in the year 1994, with a motto to create exceptional students who in turn would make an amazing society to live in. Pallavi Group of Schools are one of the best schools in Hyderabad. It has eight branches in the twin cities of Hyderabad and Secunderabad – at Bowenpally, Alwal, Boduppal, Gandipet, Saroor Nagar, Tivoli, Attapur, Keesara , Bachupally and Tirumalagiri. Our schools have pre-primary, primary, middle and senior sections with a state of art infrastructure and committed staff to reach academic benchmarks.

Pallavi Kidz

"PallaviKidz Play School embodies the overarching vision of nurturing future achievers and leaders. This establishment is a natural progression for the creators of the renowned Pallavi Group of Institutions. With the same dedication and affection that has been the hallmark of our educational institutions, PallaviKidz enters the realm of early childhood education, even as we embrace the competitive landscape of the educational sector."

Pallavi Model School

"Pallavi Model School (PMS) stands as a testament to our enduring commitment to fostering future achievers and leaders. It represents the cornerstone of the Pallavi Group's educational journey, characterized by a steadfast dedication to quality education. PMS consistently strives to provide a nurturing environment that empowers students to excel academically and develop essential life skills. Our unwavering passion for education ensures that PMS remains a beacon of excellence in the educational landscape."

Pallavi Engineering College

"Pallavi Engineering College (PEC) is a testament to the Pallavi Group's enduring pursuit of excellence in education. Our institution is committed to producing skilled professionals who are not only academically proficient but also equipped with the practical knowledge and innovation needed in the ever-evolving field of engineering. PEC's state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and industry-focused curriculum ensure that our graduates are well-prepared to meet the demands of a dynamic world. Join us at PEC as we shape the engineers and innovators of the future.
