About Pallavi International School Bhongir Branch

Pallavi Kidz – Habsiguda, the Top Preschools in Hyderabad, is a wonderful school that provides the best learning experience to your children and an opportunity to explore their interests. we believe every child has an equal potential to be great, hence the qualified teaching staff along with our dedicated non-teaching staff at Pallavi Kidz, encourage and inspire the children to become passionate learners along with their peers. It’s an absolute privilege to support every new child who comes to Pallavi Kidz School.

PALLAVI KIDZ Habsiguda was established in 2014 when children come to Pallavi Kidz, we ensure to provide them joyful early educational experience in a safe, secure and pollution-free environment. We offer classes for Playgroup(PG), Nursery, LKG(PP1), UKG(PP2).
